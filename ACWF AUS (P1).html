Welcome!

ACWF is committed to guiding newly arrived and established Chinese women to integrate into the local community. We assist them to acquire the necessary skills to make a meaningful contribution to Australian society.

ACWF encourages its members to gain a greater sense of self in an Australian cultural context, with a strategy of social engagement and joyful interaction with women of all nationalities and cultures.

The objects for which the association is established are as follow:

  • Established the Australian Chinese Women’s Entrepreneurship and Employment Foundation.
  • Develop and improve women’s education and employment skills.
  • Carry out various public welfare undertakings for women and other community cultural activities.
  • Establish and improve the functional site construction of the “Women’s House” activity center.
  • Develop various public welfare undertakings and strengthen the construction of female volunteer teams.
  • Establish protection centres for the elderly, the infirm, the sick, the disabled, the vulnerable women and children.
  • Friendly exchanges and cooperation with women’s organizations in other Australian states, international friendship groups and individuals, and women’s and children’s organizations in various countries.
  • According to the purpose of the association, accept domestic and foreign donations and volunteer services through various channels.
  • According to the purpose of the association, carry out various public welfare activities.
